INTRODUCING:

JERIMIAH BLACK

Originally from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, Black has been creating music since he was 7 years old. He started on the piano, graduated to guitar and voice, and can now compose entire orchestral pieces.

His prodigal talent led him to major in Music Theory and Composition at Carnegie Mellon University. He then went on to write film scores for horror production company, MagGot Films.

His love for music brought him to New York City, where he currently resides and regularly performs. Catch Jerimiah Black's newest album, We Thee People, being released Summer 2019.
